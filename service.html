Service

  I am from a largely Hispanic community in South Texas known as the Rio Grande Valley. Within my community, I have helped a financial advisor and a realtor using my knowledge of coding.

  For the financial advisor, I used RPA software called UiPath to automate the creation of csv files containing dozens of mutual funds daily prices spanning decades. Then the files were represented as tables where the user can view the monthly or yearly percentage of returns. This program was valuable to the financial advisor as he was able to compare performances of funds and to show the potential return for future clients.

  For the realtor, I was asked to collect data from county property records. Using Python libraries such as BeautifulSoup for web scraping, I produced a csv file containing a table with multiple features and thousands of rows. This was useful for the realtor to find patterns in when and where certain entities are willing to buy or sell a property.

  These are small contributions to a couple of members of my community but I received valuable practice applying my abilities to improve the lives of those in my community. I would like to continue expanding my impact on the community and any other community using the skills I have developed through coding.
